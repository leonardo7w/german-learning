German Adjective Endings — Cheat Sheet

Three patterns, one rule. Reference 0002. (Pairs with the Case cheat sheet 0001.)

The gender/case marker appears exactly once in the phrase — on the article or the adjective. Never both, never neither.
Which pattern?
  • WEAK after der-words: der, die, das, dieser, jeder, jener, welcher, alle, solche, manche, beide
  • MIXED after ein-words: ein, kein, mein, dein, sein, ihr, unser, euer, Ihr
  • STRONG no article (or after viel, wenig, etwas, mehr, numbers 2+)
Procedure
  • 1. Article type → pattern
  • 2. Case (subject/object/after preposition → see sheet 0001)
  • 3. Gender / number of the noun
  • 4. Read the ending off the table
